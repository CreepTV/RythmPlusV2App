Download GitHub

Features

Everything you need to experience Rhythm+ as a full-featured desktop app.

Discord Rich Presence

Show your friends on Discord what you are playing. With song title, direct link, and “Ask to Join” feature.

Custom Titlebar

Elegant dark theme titlebar with song display, profile avatar, and seamless integration into the game experience.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Full control via keyboard: navigation, zoom, fullscreen (F11), DevTools — all just a shortcut away.

Google OAuth Login

Seamless Google login directly in the app. No detours — sign in just like in the browser.

Cross-Platform

Available for Windows (Installer), Linux (AppImage), and macOS (DMG). The same experience everywhere.

Open Source

Fully open source under the MIT license. Check out the code, contribute, or fork the project.

Desktop Experience

The full Rhythm+ experience — natively on your desktop.
